AllRecipes' Classic Macaroni Salad

Description

As stated on the spaghetti page, I'm not gonna worry about copywriting for this so here's a copy and paste of an All Recipe's page.

Serves 10

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni according to box instruction in salted water
  2. combine mayo, sugar, vinegar, mustard, seasoning together
  3. stir in macaroni and vegetables and chill in the fridge
